# **App Name**: POA Retreat

## Core Features:

- Resort Overview & Highlights: A visually engaging landing page introducing the resort's unique offerings, atmosphere, and key attractions.
- Accommodation & Dining Details: Dedicated sections detailing various room types, amenities, and on-site dining experiences with high-quality images and descriptions.
- Activities & Facilities Showcase: Present an overview of available guest activities, spa services, fitness facilities, and other resort amenities.
- Interactive Photo Gallery: A browsable gallery displaying professional photographs and potentially video tours of the resort's grounds, rooms, and experiences.
- Booking Inquiry Form: A simplified contact form allowing potential guests to submit inquiries regarding availability, rates, and special requests for their stay.
- AI Guest Concierge Tool: An AI-powered chatbot tool to instantly answer frequently asked questions about the resort, its policies, and services.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A tranquil sage green (#56804D) to evoke nature and serenity, ensuring readability against lighter backgrounds.
- Background color: A very subtle, almost white, muted green (#F6F8F5) to maintain a clean, airy, and refreshing aesthetic.
- Accent color: A warm, vibrant chartreuse (#D5DA3B) used sparingly for calls to action and important highlights to draw attention effectively.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant, high-end feel that communicates luxury and style.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for excellent readability, providing a modern yet approachable counterpoint to the headlines.
- Utilize sophisticated and minimalist line-art icons that complement the resort theme, focusing on hospitality, relaxation, and nature.
- A spacious, image-centric layout with generous white space to convey a sense of calm and luxury, highlighting visual content effectively.
- Implement subtle, smooth transitions and hover effects to enhance user interaction without distracting from the tranquil atmosphere.
